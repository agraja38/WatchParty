Privacy-First Synchronization

Watch together from your own official accounts.

WatchParty syncs play, pause, seek, room chat, and a 2-person WebRTC video call. Seamless coordination without touching DRM or copying video streams.

Get Extension Setup Guide

What it syncs

  • ⚡
    Playback State

    Play, pause, seek position, and current time are synchronized instantly.

  • 💬
    Room Chat

    Send messages in real-time within the popup or the on-page overlay.

  • 📞
    2-Person Call

    WebRTC video and audio stream directly peer-to-peer to your buddy.

🚀

Features

Create a short, randomized room code, invite a friend, and keep your streaming players in step. Safe HTML5 video player integration supports YouTube and generic players, alongside passive detection for major streaming sites.

🛠️

Setup

  1. Download or clone the WatchParty repository from GitHub.
  2. Open your Chromium browser and navigate to chrome://extensions (or edge://extensions).
  3. Enable Developer Mode at the top right.
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the extension/ folder.
🔒

Privacy Summary

WatchParty does not capture, record, stream, or rehost video files or decryption keys. Rooms, peer-to-peer WebRTC signals, and temporary chat messages are coordinated securely via Firebase.
